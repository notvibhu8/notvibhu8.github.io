## üìù How to Use VoiceLICT

1. **Log In:** Open VoiceLICT and log in using your @lict.edu.np email. You will need to validate your email to activate your account.
  
2. **Submit a Concern:**
   - On the main dashboard, click the "New Submission" button.
   - Choose how you want to submit your concern: text, image, video, or voice.
   - Fill in the required details and click the "Submit" button.

3. **Track Your Concerns:**
   - After submission, use the "My Submissions" section to track the status of your concerns.
   - You will receive notifications regarding updates on your submission.
